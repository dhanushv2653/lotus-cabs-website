Our Story

Built for people tired
of unreliable rides

Lotus Cabs began with a familiar Bangalore frustration. Delayed pickups, no updates, and careless service.

After experiencing this too often one question remained. Why should reliable travel be so hard to find. So we built Lotus Cabs to do things differently. Planned rides checked vehicles and thoughtful service from everyday travel to premium journeys in Mercedes and BMW designed to feel calm predictable and cared for.
